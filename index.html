Your child is unique - so are we.

Since its foundation in 1990, our day care center has been managed by people like you - parents. This means having responsibilities, but makes for a familial atmosphere. Your child will feel right at home with us.

A Safe Haven

Professional but loving care ensures that our children (aged 0-6) can grow in a nurturing environment. There is no shortage of teachers here - barring emergencies, 3-5 teachers are with your child at any time.

A Place to Play & Learn

A wide rage of activities ensure that there's always something fun to be learned - be it making things, music, cooking, baking, gymnastics, swimming or a morning in the woods.

A Playground of Possibilities

Kids also need time and room to just play, explore and grow. Our facilities offer them just that. There are multiple rooms, an attractive garden and plenty of space out front in a quiet blind alley.

Out and About

Weather permitting, our teachers take the kids on trips to the woods, the nearby city center, to see a play at the theater or even to learn about the police and fire station. The world is ours to explore!
